HOW CAN WE PREVENT FIRE?

tip description
Smoke alarms can signal when there is a fire in your house. Installing these alarms around your house is very crucial for it may make everyone in your house alert which can prevent a fire from occuring.
Installing sprinklers in your houses may be an easier way to stop fires from happening. If your house catches on fire, the sprinklers will put out the fire by 96% before the firefighters even arrive.
Of course, we always cook in our households, but do we cook safely? We should always be aware that we should cook safely. Keeping any types of cloth away from our opened stoves is already a way on how to prevent fires in the house. We should also make sure to keep away young childen from the open stove.
Having an emergency go bag is very important for it may contain a lot of the things you need for evactuation if your house burnes down by a lot. It must contain the essentails that we need in our daily life and our important legal documents.
Lighters, matches, candles, all of these should be out of the children's reach for they can think these are toys and light up any flammable items around the house.
